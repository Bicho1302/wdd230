Spanish Fork Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

Name: Spanish Fork Chamber of Commerce

Reason: The name represents the city/region where the chamber operates and highlights its focus on supporting businesses and the community.

Site Purpose

The website will serve the following purposes:

  • Provide essential resources and contact information for local businesses.
  • Promote business-to-business networking opportunities and events.
  • Act as a central hub for community development and regional economic updates.

Scenarios

These are some common questions site visitors may ask:

  • What events are scheduled to promote networking in the community?
  • How can I join the chamber as a member?
  • Where can I access economic development resources for my business?

Color Schema

The following colors will be used to reflect the chamber's mission and professionalism:

  • Blue (#3a6ea5): For headers and navigation background, symbolizing trust and stability.
  • Brown (#5b3e23): Accent color for buttons and highlights, representing reliability.

Typography

The following fonts will be used:

  • Roboto: For body text, ensuring readability and a modern feel.
  • Merriweather: For headings, providing a bold and professional appearance.
